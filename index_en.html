About Us

CONTINENTAL COMMERCIAL CORPORATION (CCC) is a holding company with more than 30 years of experience in the generation of international business

INVERSIONES ZULUAGA SA (IZSA) It is the leading company, within the holding, dedicated to the trading of agricultural products and by-products.

Based in the Republic of Panama, it works as a financial and commercial center for the export of products from Latin America, especially Argentina and Brazil, to the People's Republic of China and Indonesia.

We have a wide network of professionals who deal directly with producers and cooperatives of different products.

Mission and Values

Our main commitment is to build long-term business relationships with an ethical and reliable foundation.
The knowledge and respect towards the cultural differences of the clients is our added value.
Competitiveness and profitability mark our objectives, along with innovation and versatility to create solutions tailored to the needs.
