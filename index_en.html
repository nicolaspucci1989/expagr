About Us

CONTINENTAL COMMERCIAL CORPORATION (CCC) is a holding company with more than 30 years of experience in the generation of international business

INVERSIONES ZULUAGA SA (IZSA) It is the leading company within the holding company, dedicated to the marketing of agricultural products and by-products.

Based in Panama City, it works as a financial and commercial center for the export of products from Latin America, especially Argentina and Brazil, to the People's Republic of China and Indonesia.

We have a wide network of professionals who deal directly with producers and cooperatives of different products.

    Procedures

    1. Interested Customer sends LOI with complete direct buyer data
    2. Seller issues SCO or FCO
    3. Buyer returns signed and sealed FCO
    4. Seller issues and sends draft contract
    5. Once the contract of both parties is reviewed, a definitive contract with digital signatures is signed.
    6. The buyer issues BCL in his Bank or opens LC in favor of the seller, The LC will not be released until the buyer's bank receives the shipping documents.
    7. The seller issues PB 2% in favor of the buyer as a guarantee of contract fulfillment.
    8. Seller issues product POP and other certificates specified in the contract, including SGS certificate.
    9. The seller makes the shipment
    10. The buyer releases the LC payment against the required documents.
